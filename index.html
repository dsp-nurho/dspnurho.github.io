Discover Delta Sigma Pi



Delta Sigma Pi prepares business students for successful professional careers, but also instills values of service and principled leadership that characterize Deltasigs in all phases of life. With more than 260,000 initiates, our members make a difference in their workplaces and communities throughout the United States and around the world. From the day you pledge Delta Sigma Pi, you’ll have access to quality professional training, leadership education, service opportunities, and social activities through your local chapter and at regional and national conferences. And the benefits you receive from Delta Sigma Pi membership don’t stop at graduation. Opportunities to network, get involved in high-impact service, and receive ongoing professional development are yours for life.
